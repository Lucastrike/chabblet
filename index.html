About Chabblet

Chabblet is a unique, multi-purpose 'Communications and Activities Aid' powered by TouchPhrase® Technology.

Simple to use, Chabblet has many key communication and activities features that are instant, simple and easy to use requiring 'one touch'.

Chabblet is suitable for all ages and abilities.

Chabblet's Key Features

  • Pre-set Phrases and Quick Statetments for One-2-One communication.
  • The 'About Me' Album to communicate a client's likes/dislikes, food/drink, pain charts, medical information etc. Can also be used for activities participation, enhancing quality of life and well-being.
  • The ‘Drawing Board’ offers a creative way to communicate via symbols, pictures or writing, perfect for facilitating engagement and cognitive rehabilitation.
  • The ‘Family and Friends’ and ‘Emergency Text Facility’ offer unique ways that Clients can stay in touch using Pre-set Phrases.
  • Ability to upload other apps and activities to suit particular requirements.

Who is Chabblet for?

Chabblet is designed for the wide variety of Medical Staff, Therapy Professionals, Care Staff and Activity Coordinators who work throughout the Health and Social Care Sector.

Chabblet is also suitable for anyone, of any age where the ability to communicate has become resticted or removed. It can also be used by Clients, their Families and Friends within the Healthcare Facilities, at Home or Out and About.
