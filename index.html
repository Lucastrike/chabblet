Chabblet's Features

  • Pre-set Phrases and Quick Statetments for One-2-One communication.
  • The 'About Me' Album which communicates a client's likes/dislikes, food/drink, pain charts, medical information etc. Can also be used to improve participation, enhancing quality of life and well-being.
  • The ‘Drawing Board’ offers a creative way communicate via symbols, pictures or writing, perfect for facilitating engagement and cognitive rehabilitation.
  • The ‘Family and Friends’ and ‘Emergency Text Facility’ offer unique ways that clients can stay in touch using Pre-set Phrases.

Who is the Chabblet for?

The Chabblet is designed for use by Care Staff, Activity Coordinators and other Professionals (and their clients) to:

  • Aid and Improve ‘Communication’ with their clients, especially in challenging circumstances.
  • Be an ‘Activities’ Aid for Activity Coordinators to improve a client’s engagement, promote Cognitive Rehabilitation or assist in Reminiscence Therapy.
  • Improve their Efficiency in carrying out their roles.
  • Save Staff Time, Frustrations and improve Job Satisfaction.
  • Save Operational and Overhead Costs for their Organisation.
  • Provide a Competitive Advantage for their Organisation in the use of Communications Technology.
